Everyday Tech

Tech is everywhere — here's some fun about the stuff we all use.

Why did the phone wear glasses? It lost its contacts!
What's a spider's favorite hobby? Making websites!
Why did the computer go to the doctor? It caught a virus!
My autocorrect is driving me nuts — it can go to heck!
Why was the TV shy? It didn't want to stream itself!
What's the biggest lie ever? "I agree to the terms."
Why was the laptop tired? Too many sleep-mode naps!
